# Swedish Beginner Practice Kit

Use these exercises after reading the matching textbook pages. Check the answers only after writing or speaking first.

## 1. Alphabet Warm-Up

Say the Swedish alphabet aloud once. Then say these words slowly:

```text
hej
tack
bok
dator
kaffe
språk
förstår
svenska
```

Focus:
- Keep `å`, `ä`, and `ö` separate from `a` and `o`.
- Notice long/short vowel patterns: `tak` versus `tack`.

## 4. Pronoun Drill

Translate into Swedish.

1. I
2. you
3. he
4. she
5. we
6. you all
7. they

Then make three sentences:

```text
Jag ...
Du ...
Vi ...
```

## 5. Verb Drill

Fill the gap with the Swedish present tense verb.

| English | Swedish |
|---|---|
| I am called Rony. | Jag ___ Rony. |
| I come from India. | Jag ___ från Indien. |
| I speak English. | Jag ___ engelska. |
| I understand a little. | Jag ___ lite. |
| I live in Stockholm. | Jag ___ i Stockholm. |
| I study Swedish. | Jag ___ svenska. |

## 6. Noun Drill

Add the article `en` or `ett`.

1. ___ bok
2. ___ dator
3. ___ språk
4. ___ land
5. ___ kaffe
6. ___ penna

Then write the definite form:

```text
en bok -> boken
ett språk -> språket
```

## 7. Dialogue Practice

Write your own version.

```text
A: Hej! Vad heter du?
B: Jag heter ______. Och du?
A: Jag heter ______. Varifrån kommer du?
B: Jag kommer från ______. Vad talar du för språk?
A: Jag talar ______ och lite svenska.
B: Trevligt att träffas!
A: Trevligt att träffas!
```

Speak it twice:
- First slowly and clearly.
- Then at a natural speed.

## 8. Translation Practice

Translate into Swedish.

1. Hi, my name is Rony.
2. I come from India.
3. I live in Stockholm.
4. I speak English and a little Swedish.
5. Do you speak Swedish?
6. What time is it?
7. I would like coffee.
8. Thank you.

## 9. Mini Writing Task

Write 6-8 Swedish sentences about yourself.

Use:
- `Jag heter ...`
- `Jag kommer från ...`
- `Jag bor i ...`
- `Jag talar ...`
- `Jag studerar ...`
- `Jag gillar ...`
